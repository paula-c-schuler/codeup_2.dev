Quick Fact

The Superlaser is the Death Star's main weapon. The Superlaser is created by coordinating eight lasers manned by 168 gunners located in the Eye of the ship. The Eye of the ship is a circular concave shape built around a focusing crystal. The Eye has no defenses and requires military backup for protection.
