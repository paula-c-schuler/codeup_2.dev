Ten FAQs about National Parks

How many people visited the national parks in 2014?
Approximately 293,000,000
How do I obtain a park pass?
You can obtain a park pass by visiting your nearest park.
Where is the largest national park?
In Alaska, Wrangell-St. Elias National Park and Preserve, more than 13 million acres.
Where is the smallest national park?
Pennsylvania, Thaddeus Kosciuszko National Monument, 1/5th of an acre.
How do I make a camping reservation?
Visit Recreation.gov.
What are concessions?
Concessionaires provide park visitors with lodging, transportation, food services,
shops, and other services. More than 500 NPS concession contracts in more than
120 different park units) vary in size from small, family-owned businesses to
national/international corporations.
How many employees work the national parks?
Including part-time, seasonal and full-time: Approximately 22,000.
How many volunteers work in the national parks?
Approximately 221,000.
Which park is the most visited park in 2014?
The Golden Gate National Park.
How many parks are in the national park system?
There are 59 parks.
